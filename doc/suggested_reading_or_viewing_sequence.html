This is the suggested sequence to read ivy presentations / watch ivy videos

The powerpoint presentations, their .pdf equivalents, and the sample output are found in two places.

·        On github.com along with the source, at https://github.com/Hitachi-Data-Systems/ivy

·        On the HDS community at https://community.hds.com/groups/ivy
